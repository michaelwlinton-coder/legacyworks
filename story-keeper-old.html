The 6–8 week program

A guided journey, not a single shoot.

The best stories don’t come out in a single sitting. We pace the work over weeks so memories surface, deepen, and connect.

Discovery

A 60-minute family call. We learn the subject’s life, the family’s priorities, and design a custom interview arc.

Weekly interviews

6–8 one-hour video sessions led by a trained Story Coach. Conversational, gentle, and rich with the questions that actually matter.

Editing & design

Our editors craft each session into a polished episode. Designers transform the transcript and family photos into a beautiful book.

Delivery & legacy

Episodes go to a private family channel. Books, USB keepsake, and a 15–20 minute Legacy Highlight Film arrive at your door.

Every Story Keeper package includes

Everything you need. Nothing you have to manage.

  • Discovery call with your dedicated Story Coach to understand the subject, the family, and what matters most.
  • 6–8 weekly 1-hour interviews, gently guided and beautifully filmed over Zoom or Teams.
  • All editing handled in-house — color, sound, music, captions, transitions, the whole craft.
  • Private family video channel on YouTube where every episode is delivered as it’s finished.
  • Family photo collection & restoration — we gather, scan, and weave old photos into the story.
  • Professionally designed Life Story Book — digital edition + a printed hardcover keepsake.
  • USB keepsake with every digital asset, raw footage, and finished file in one place.
  • 15–20 minute Legacy Highlight Film — the cinematic short you’ll show at birthdays and family reunions.
