The commission

Cinema, not video.

Story Legacy is not an interview series. It is a feature documentary — directed, scored, photographed, and edited with the craft you’d expect from a film festival premiere.

We come to your family’s home, the places that shaped your loved one, the rooms where the most important moments happened. We interview the people who knew them best. We score it with original music. We present it on a screen, in a room, with the people who matter most.

This is what grandchildren show their grandchildren.

Every Story Legacy commission includes

A bespoke production. A finished masterpiece.

  • Dedicated Producer + Creative Director guiding every creative decision from first call to final screening.
  • Feature-length documentary — 45 to 90 minutes, fully directed and edited to broadcast standard.
