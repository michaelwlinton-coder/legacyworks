From your kitchen table
to a finished film — in four steps.

I

Get set up — Day 1

Follow the gear guide, download Riverside, and send your loved one their first invite. Most families complete this in under an hour. You could record your first session this weekend.

II

Begin the conversations — Week 1

Open the 52-week question guide and read the first session's questions. Then sit down — or set up a video call — and ask them. Record for 30–45 minutes. You've just done more than most families ever will.

III
