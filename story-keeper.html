When was the last time you heard your grandmother's voice? Not a photo of her — her voice. The way she paused before a story. The way she laughed at her own joke before the punchline.

Most of us can't answer that. Not because we didn't love them — but because no one ever sat them down, pressed record, and asked. Story Keeper is us doing that for you, before the chance is gone.

Why families wait — and then it's too late

You've meant to do this for years.

Everyone intends to capture their parent's story. Almost no one does. The camera feels awkward. The right questions don't come. Life gets busy. And then one day the chance is simply gone — and what's left is a handful of photos and the ache of everything you didn't ask.

It's not a failure of love. It's that no one ever made it easy. That's the entire reason Story Keeper exists.

"We didn't know how much we didn't know about Mom — until we finally sat down and asked."
By week three, nearly every family tells us the same thing: they're hearing stories they'd never heard in a lifetime of Sunday dinners. That's what happens when someone who knows how to ask is the one asking.
How it works

You do nothing but be there.

No filming. No editing. No awkward questions. A trained Story Coach handles everything — you just help your loved one show up.

1

We listen first

A 60-minute call with your family to learn who your loved one is and design an interview arc around the life they've lived.

2

We interview them

Six to eight gentle, guided video conversations led by a trained Story Coach. Warm, unhurried, and richer than you'd believe.

3

We craft it

Our editors shape every session into a polished episode. Our designers turn transcripts and old photos into a hardcover book.

4

You keep it forever

A private film series, a printed Life Story Book, a USB keepsake, and a Legacy Highlight Film — delivered to your door.

For your family history

The primary source, captured for you.

You’ve done the research — the tree, the records, the years of work. Story Keeper captures the one source you can’t retrieve from any archive: the living testimony behind it all. We interview, film, and transcribe every session, and preserve it to the standards the oral-history field is built on.

The result sits alongside your research as a citable, searchable, first-person source — the voice behind the record, kept with the record.

See our approach to preservation → How we preserve →
  • Preservation master — one high-quality original, kept unchanged
  • Word-for-word transcripts — linked to the footage, never AI-rewritten
  • A documented record — who, when, where, and what was covered
  • Your rights, your access — you decide who can view and share it
