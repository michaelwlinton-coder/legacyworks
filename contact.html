Common questions

Before you book.

How long is a discovery call?

Twenty minutes. We hold to that. You’ll leave knowing exactly which tier fits your family and what the next step looks like.

Is there a charge for the call?

No. Discovery calls are always complimentary, regardless of which tier you’re considering.

My loved one is in declining health. Can you start quickly?

Yes. Tell us when you book. Story Keeper interviews can begin within a week. For urgent Story Legacy commissions, our Creative Director will respond personally within 24 hours.

Can you work with families outside the US?

Yes. Story Starter and Story Keeper can be delivered remotely anywhere. Story Legacy productions are currently US-based, with international commissions accepted on a case-by-case basis.

What if I’m not sure which tier is right?

That’s exactly what the discovery call is for. Most families come in unsure. Twenty minutes later they know.
